Dessert Recipes

Chocolate Brownies

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/3 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp baking powder

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a baking pan.
  2. Melt butter and mix in sugar, eggs, and vanilla.
  3. Stir in cocoa, flour, salt, and baking powder.
  4. Spread batter in pan and bake for 20-25 minutes.
  5. Let cool before cutting into squares.

Fruit Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup strawberries, sliced
  • 1 cup grapes, halved
  • 1 cup pineapple chunks
  • 1 banana, sliced
  • 1 orange, peeled and segmented
  • 1 tbsp honey (optional)
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice

Steps:

  1. Combine all fruits in a large bowl.
  2. Drizzle with honey and lemon juice.
  3. Toss gently to mix.
  4. Chill before serving.
